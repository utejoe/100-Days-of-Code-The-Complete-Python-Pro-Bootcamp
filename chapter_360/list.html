Recipe: Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk

For the Filling

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and milk to form a dough.
  3. Roll out the dough and spread melted butter on top.
  4. Sprinkle the cinnamon and brown sugar mixture over the dough.
  5. Roll up the dough and cut into slices.
  6. Place the slices on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Let cool before serving.
